About Namsan Tower

Namsan Tower, officially named the N Seoul Tower, is a well-known communication and observation tower located on Namsan Mountain in Seoul, South Korea. Built in 1969 and opened in 1980, it serves as both a broadcasting tower for various media outlets and a popular landmark and tourist attraction that offers panoramic views of the city. The tower is famous for its observatories, restaurants, and the "Locks of Love" area.

Travel Tips

  1. Time commitment: Plan to spend at least 1 to 2 hours to fully explore the park and tombs.
  2. Admission fee: Be prepared to pay a small entrance fee; it is advisable to check the current cost before you visit, as there may be discounts for certain visitors.
  3. Spring visit: If visiting in the spring, the park is especially beautiful with cherry blossoms. Wear comfortable shoes: You will need to walk uphill from the parking or bus terminal to the main tomb area.
  4. Enjoy the surroundings: Take time to relax and enjoy the park's green spaces. Don't miss the nearby riverside views for a peaceful break.
