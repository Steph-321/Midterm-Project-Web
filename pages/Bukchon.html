About Bukchon Hanok Village

Bukchon Hanok Village is a unique blend of history, culture, and modern living. It offers visitors a chance to explore traditional Korean architecture and experience the serene beauty of a bygone era, all while being situated in the heart of bustling Seoul. Whether you're interested in history, architecture, or simply enjoying a peaceful stroll, Bukchon Hanok Village is a must-visit destination.

Travel Tips

  1. Best Time to Visit: Visit during weekdays or early afternoons to avoid crowds and enjoy a quieter experience.
  2. Transportation: Use the subway system, with Anguk Station being the closest and most accessible.
  3. Accommodation: Consider staying in hanok guesthouses like Rakkojae for an authentic experience.
  4. Activities: Explore the village, rent a hanbok, and enjoy the traditional atmosphere.
  5. Dining: Sample local cuisine at cafes and restaurants within the village.
