Explore Japan

Discover the perfect blend of ancient traditions and cutting-edge modernity.

Top Tourist Destinations

Mount Fuji

Japan’s tallest mountain, Mount Fuji offers breathtaking views and spans Shizuoka and Yamanashi prefectures.

Tokyo Tower

Tokyo Tower stands as a post-war symbol and a popular tourist landmark in Japan’s capital city.

Kyoto Monument

Kyoto’s monument cluster preserves centuries-old cultural and spiritual relics from Japan’s rich historical past.

Himeji Castle

Himeji Castle is a well-preserved ancient fortress in Hyogo Prefecture, west of Tokyo.

Itsukushima Shrine

Itsukushima Shrine on Miyajima Island is a revered Shinto site known for its floating torii gate.

Fushimi Inari Shrine

Fushimi Inari Shrine features iconic orange torii gates and fox statues symbolizing fortune and health.

Shinsekai

Shinsekai is an old Osaka district with nostalgic charm, centered around the Tsutenkaku Tower.

Nara Park

Nara Park is home to friendly deer and the historic Todaiiji Temple, a favorite among visitors.

Arashiyama Bamboo Grove

Arashiyama Bamboo Grove in Kyoto offers serene trails surrounded by towering bamboo stalks.

Kenrokuen Garden

Kenrokuen Garden in Kanazawa is a historic landscape garden once part of Kanazawa Castle, open since the 19th century.
