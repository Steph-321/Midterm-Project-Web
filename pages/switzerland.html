Explore Switzerland

Explore Switzerland’s tall mountains, clear lakes, and calm villages.

Top Tourist Destinations

Zermatt and the Matterhorn

Zermatt is a peaceful Alpine village with car-free streets and stunning views of the iconic Matterhorn.

Jungfrau Region

The Jungfrau Region offers breathtaking Alpine scenery and iconic peaks like the Eiger, Mönch, and Jungfrau.

Lucerne

Lucerne is a lakeside city with mountain views, rich history, and plenty of outdoor adventures.

Interlaken

Interlaken sits between two lakes and offers thrilling mountain adventures like paragliding and canyoning.

Geneva

Geneva blends lakeside beauty with global significance as home to the UN, Red Cross, and rich history.

Lauterbrunnen Valley

Lauterbrunnen Valley enchants with 72 waterfalls, including Staubbach Falls, in a serene Alpine setting.

Bern

Bern, Switzerland’s capital, charms with its UNESCO-listed Old Town and the hourly show at Zytglogge Tower.

Zurich

Zurich blends financial prestige with cultural depth, featuring historic Old Town and world-class art museums.

St. Moritz

St. Moritz is a luxury Alpine resort famed for world-class winter sports and summer mountain adventures.

Lugano

Lugano blends Swiss and Italian charm with lakeside beauty, Mediterranean flair, and serene Parco Ciani.
