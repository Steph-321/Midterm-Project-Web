Explore France

Discover the beauty of France — from its romantic cities to its delicious food and rich culture.

Top Tourist Destinations

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower, built for the 1889 World Fair, now stands as Paris’s iconic 330-meter landmark.

Musée du Louvre

The Louvre in Paris houses iconic masterpieces like the Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo sculpture.

Château de Versailles

Château de Versailles showcases France’s royal legacy and grandeur from the reign of Louis XIV to XVI.

Côte d'Azur

The Côte d'Azur is France’s chic Mediterranean coast, famed for its blue waters and glamorous resorts.

Mont Saint-Michel

Mont Saint-Michel rises from Normandy’s coast, a mystical island landmark crowned by medieval walls and abbey.

Castles of the Loire Valley

The Loire Valley, a UNESCO site, features fairy-tale castles and lush landscapes known as the “Garden of France.”

Cathédrale Notre-Dame de Chartres

Chartres Cathedral has inspired visitors for centuries with its Gothic architecture and spiritual grandeur.

Provence

Provence enchants with lavender fields, olive groves, and hilltop villages that inspired artists like Cézanne and Picasso.

Alsace Villages

Alsace’s charming villages feature pastel timbered houses, cobblestone streets, and scenic hills near the Rhine.

Carcassonne

Carcassonne is a fortified medieval city with towers and ramparts that evoke a fairy-tale atmosphere.
