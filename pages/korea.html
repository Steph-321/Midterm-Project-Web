Explore Korea

Experience a dynamic fusion of tradition and technology, from serene palaces to neon-lit streets.

Top Tourist Destinations

GyeongBokgung Palace

A historic royal palace with museums and traditional architecture.

Namsan Seoul Tower

A popular tower offering panoramic views of Seoul from Namsan Park.

Insadong

A lively street filled with tea houses, art shops, and local crafts.

National Museum of Korea

Korea’s largest museum with exhibits from ancient to modern times.

Bukchon Hanok Village

A charming neighborhood of traditional Korean houses in central Seoul.

Bulguksa Temple

A UNESCO-listed temple with beautiful pagodas and historic shrines.

Haedong Yonggungsa Temple

A seaside temple with stunning views and unique cliffside architecture.

Haeundae Beach

A lively beach in Busan known for festivals and white sand shores.

Hallasan Mountain

Jeju Island’s highest peak, perfect for hiking and nature views.

Tumuli Park

A historic park in Gyeongju with ancient burial mounds from the Silla era.
